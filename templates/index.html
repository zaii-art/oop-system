System Features

  • Product Management: Organize products with accurate pricing.
  • Smart Pricing Rules: Set prices automatically and maintain consistency.
  • Easy Discount Creation: Create fixed or percentage discounts for products.
  • Scheduled Promotions: Run timed promotions effortlessly.
  • Automatic Price Updates: Calculate final prices without changing original values.
  • Insights & Reports: Track discounts, promotions, and performance.
  • Role-Based Access: Admin and staff access for secure management.

Description

A comprehensive system designed to efficiently manage product pricing, create and track discounts, and schedule promotional periods. It allows businesses to automate price adjustments, apply special offers, and ensure accurate and consistent pricing across all products. With this system, businesses can enhance sales strategies, streamline promotions, and provide customers with timely discounts and offers.
